"Technology is the best when it brings people together"
~Matt Mullenweg, WordPress Founder.
We bring people together in the name of technology

No idea if we actually do, but we sure plan to! :P


We have got this immense opportunity from our school Computer Science Department to set up this club and we really are thankful to them. Our goal here is to educate each other about technology, the familiar name in Economical Development. We learn, compete and collaborate with each other in this club. Our motive is to set up a technology based environment in our school.


We are comprised of four departments, namely Competitive Programming, Development, Quizzing and Multimedia. You can and should check them out and choose the one(or more ;) ) that interests you!


We would love to be a body of school that contributes to it's welfare and also that contributes to success for each of the students individually.

~DPS Ruby Park Tech Club Core Committee.
